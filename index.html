Get out to play!

Who Plays?

People from all over Sault Ste. Marie, Ontario get together for some fun-packed Pokemon Adventures. With over 150 current plays signed up in our forums, and the team is growing daily, you cannot miss out on the fun! Get out and meet some new friends! Play, compete, battle with or against others in gyms, or get together for the raids! Including the new in-person Shadow Raids!

What we do!

Party up!

We can get together by using our forum board on this site or by using our social media! Togehter we can create parties and complete the party challenges, as well as earning our bonus items. Not to mention the power boost when you go up against a raid!

Raids!

Get a raid group together and get out into the Sault to hunt down some of the toughest foes known to Pokemon! From legendary Mewtwo, to the iconic Diagla, you are going to need some friends to help you challenge these Pocket Monsters and be able to capture them for your own collection!

Dress up and Explore!

Get dressed up and join our ranks! We love going on outtings dressed as our favourite characters from the show! Everyone enjoys a smile, and doesn't it feel great picking up a compliment and bonding over your favourite Pokemon!

Add to your Collection!

Collection!!! Thats what its all about! Isn't it? We are all on a journey to complete our Pokemon collection. Trying to fill the Pokedex? Perfectdex? Luckydex? So are we! Trading and helping eachother out any way possible is our main goal! Whether you join our team to go out with the numbers, or to play solo and boast about your hunts - we would love to see you around!
